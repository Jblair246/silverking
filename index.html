LIMITLESS CLIMATE ACTION GROUP

The official name of the organization is Limitless Climate Action Group

VISION

To create a sustainable future through proactive climate action, environmental stewardship, and empowerment of communities.

MISSION

To inspire and mobilize individuals and communities towards meaningful action against climate change by promoting environmental awareness, education, and sustainable living.

CORE VALUES

Integrity, Sustainability, Accountability, Inclusiveness, Innovation, and Collaboration

OBJECTIVES

Educate the public on climate change and sustainability. Advocate for policies that promote environmental protection. Engage in community-driven climate action projects. Promote renewable energy and reduce reliance on fossil fuels. Foster partnerships with local and global environmental organizations.

MEMBERSHIP

Open to all individuals and organizations committed to the mission and values of the group, without discrimination
